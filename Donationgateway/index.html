Floods In Bihar, India Alert

Bihar is India's most flood-prone state, with 76% population in the North Bihar living under the recurring threat of flood devastation. Bihar makes up 16.5% of India's flood affected area and 22.1% of India's flood affected population. About 73.06% of Bihar's geographical area, ie 68,800 square kilometres (26,600 sq mi) out of 94,160 square kilometres (36,360 sq mi), is flood affected.

Forest Fires In Uttarakhand, India Alert!

Uttarakhand has been burning for 4 days now with over 46 bush fires. So far only 2 deaths have been reported, but the fire is still nowhere under control. A report by the Times Of India has stated that Uttarakhand has lost nearly 51.43 hectares of forest cover and the forest department alone has incurred a loss of over 1 lakh.

Uttaranchal is a state in the northern part of India.
